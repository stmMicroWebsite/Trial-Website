STM32 Education

The Sensortile miniaturized bluetooth LE platform has been conceived to help the developer creating a Physical Activity Monitor thanks to a dedicated selection of libraries that help tracking activity, carry position, pedometer, calories burned, etc. The sensortile platform is inteded both to help the developer collecting data (rapid prototyping) and as a starting point for a complete Phycical Activity Monitor design.
